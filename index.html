About Me

Introduction

I'm Phillip Tritz, a passionate developer focused on real-time data systems and AI transaction monitoring. I thrive on building efficient, user-friendly interfaces and robust backend solutions.

My Journey

My journey into technology began with a simple curiosity about how computers work. What started as tinkering with old hardware in my garage evolved into a deep fascination with system architecture and data flow. Through my experiences at Shaw and Fido, I discovered that technology isn't just about writing code or fixing systems—it's about understanding people's needs and crafting solutions that make their lives easier.

Today, I find myself at the intersection of hardware expertise and software innovation. Whether it's optimizing database performance, securing network infrastructure, or developing intuitive user interfaces, I approach each project with the same goal: to create reliable, efficient solutions that stand the test of time. What drives me is the satisfaction of seeing businesses transform and grow through the thoughtful application of technology.
